# Contractor Local SEO Schema Generator

A free tool for home service contractors to generate valid LocalBusiness structured data (schema.org JSON-LD) for their websites.

Proper schema markup helps Google understand your business, improves local search visibility, and supports Map Pack rankings.

## What This Tool Does

Paste your business details into the generator and get a ready-to-install JSON-LD schema block for:

- Plumbers
- HVAC contractors
- Septic services
- Roofing contractors
- Drain cleaning services
- Any home service business

## Why Schema Matters for Contractors

Google uses structured data to verify your business information. Contractors with complete, accurate schema markup signal stronger relevance for local searches — which directly impacts Map Pack rankings and inbound call volume.

Most contractor websites have no schema, incorrect schema, or outdated schema. This tool fixes that in under 2 minutes.

## How to Use

1. Open `schema-generator.html` in any browser
2. Fill in your business details
3. Copy the generated JSON-LD code
4. Paste it into your website's `<head>` tag or via a plugin like WPCode

## What Gets Generated

- `LocalBusiness` schema with contractor-specific service types
- `areaServed` with zip code or city targeting
- `hasOfferCatalog` with your services listed
- `aggregateRating` block (if you have reviews)
- `contactPoint` with phone and service hours
